## Numbers

Numbers are easier to read than words, so use them where possible. Some examples:

> - 1—9
> - 10—999,999
> - Use a word for people or animals upwards from 1 million eg 22.3 million
> - 100s of them
> - 5 kg
> - 50KB
> - 10am
> - $2
> - 75%
> - Section 10
> - 19th century
> - 1980s
> - 25 people went to the launch
